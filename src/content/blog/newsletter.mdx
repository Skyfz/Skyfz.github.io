## Mailchimp powed newsletters

Create multiple newsletter forms with list & tag support.

You can configure your newsletters in the CMS under **Settings > Contact, Social & Forms.** Configure your global newsletter settings and create lists you can open by an anchor link.

Set your mailchimp credentials in your .env and in your netlify deploy settings

```
MAILCHIMP_API_KEY=XXX
MAILCHIMP_SERVER_PREFIX=us21	
MAILCHIMP_LIST_ID=XXX
```
